How safe are my shipments with First Fly Express?

While all efforts are made to ensure zero error service, we also have a ‘Comprehensive Risk Coverage Policy’, which insures your shipment against any unforeseen event such as loss or damage during transit. Your cargo is fully protected.

What shipments cannot be carried by you?

We do not carry the following items:

  • Stamped and prepaid postal envelopes and parcels
  • Precious stones, gems and jewelry
  • Uncrossed (bearer) drafts / cheque, currency and coins
  • Poison
  • Firearms, explosives and military equipment
  • Hazardous and radioactive material
  • Foodstuff and liquor
  • Any pornographic material
  • Hazardous chemical items

All items that infringe the Indian Postal Act of 1898 and all restricted items as per the guidelines of IATA.

Can I book cash, precious stones or jewellery?

Carrying or booking valuables such as these is illegal as they are items banned under the ‘Postal Regulation Act’. We do not carry the same and hold no responsibility if booked without our knowledge. We strictly advise against including such items.

Can your representative visit me for a discussion?

Please send a mail to sales@firstflyexpress.com (bearing the subject ‘Business Call’). Mention the time and date convenient for you along with your contact details, and our representative will visit you promptly.

Can I change the address of a shipment that is not yet delivered?

Yes, get in touch with the booking branch. If the shipment has not been delivered, the needful shall be done. Please provide written intimation within 48 hours of booking. Alternatively, email sales@firstflyexpress.com with subject ‘Change Address’, including your contact details and the new address.

How can I track my shipment in real-time?

You can track your consignment using our Track n Trace tool. Simply visit the Track n Trace page and enter your Airway Bill Number or reference ID to get real-time status updates.
